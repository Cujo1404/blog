  • What is Emotional Intelligence?
    Emotional intelegnece for me is the depth, understanding and respect of another human beings feels. it is having the ability to empathise and be aware. to magian your own and other emotions with grace. but also a high level of self awareness, to be able to withhold your emotions and control your output. keep a level head and stay balanced.

  • How is it different to IQ?
    EQ is typically considered to be connected to the intuitive self, a gut feeling rather than a logical thought.

  • Why is it important?
    because more people feel heard and seen in a workplace when people can actively listen to one another. it helps build strong relationships and friendships. EQ strengthens your entire social life from the ground up. for me its been transformational.
