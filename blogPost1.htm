What are the differences between relative, absolute, and fixed positioning?

Relative positioning
I like to think of relative positioning as it relates to its natural order. Say its the 3rd block on the page, using relative postioning +/- the item is still grounded to its natural place, only adjusted or corrected.

Absolute positioning
In a game of chess the Queen can move anywhere on the board. This super power ability is how I like to think of absolute positioning, input the x & y coordinates and your queen can be anywhere she desires.

Fixed positioning
Something that uses fixed positioning is always visible inside the web browser, even if the user scrolls down. This is beacuse the block is fixed to a particular point in relation to the visible area of the browser window.
